Why Coding?

I'm an archivist at Special Collections. We're working on expanding our web archiving program, which is how I got interested in coding. There are so many limitations to what the tools we work with can do, but I'm not always sure when the limitations are really the tools, and when they're just my lack of knowledge! Taking the Front-End Development Class should hopefully be a great way to build my skills.

Why Northwest Arkansas?

I'm from Alabama, but school and work have taken me around the county a bit. I'm excited to have settled in Northwest Arkansas, and excited about all the potential opportunities in the region.

  • World-class museums and cultural institutions
  • (Relatively) reasonable traffic
  • All four seasons!
  • Beautiful mountains
  • Thriving food scene
  • Welcoming community
  • And--as I'm increasingly learning--a very active and growing tech and start-up community
